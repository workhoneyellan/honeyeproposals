About Me

The Strategist Behind Your Winning Bid

I am Honey E., a proposal writing specialist with over two years of focused experience helping businesses compete — and win — in government and private sector procurement.

Since 2024, I have written and managed 179 proposals across federal, state, municipal, and private channels, resulting in 27 awarded contracts and a consistent ~15% win rate across competitive bid environments.

My work spans a wide range of industries and solicitation types: from janitorial and cleaning service contracts to product supply bids for uniforms and equipment, and from professional services proposals to translation and transcription service RFPs. I understand that each industry speaks a different language — and I know how to write in all of them.

I manage the full proposal lifecycle: opportunity research, compliance review, writing, formatting, submission, and follow-up. Every proposal I submit is treated as a strategic business case — not just a document.

Real Work, Real Results

Case Studies

Janitorial & Cleaning Services — Municipal RFP

Government Building Janitorial & Maintenance Bid

A janitorial services company with solid field experience had been submitting bids without success. Their proposals lacked proper compliance documentation, scope alignment, and the structured formatting that procurement officers expect.

Performed a full RFP analysis, rewrote the proposal to align with the agency’s scope and evaluation criteria, compiled all required certifications and attachments, and managed the full SAM.gov submission process including post-submission follow-up.

Outcome

Contract awarded — the client’s first successful government contract after multiple unsuccessful attempts.

Translation & Transcription Services — State RFP

Language Services Proposal for Government Agency

A translation and transcription services provider needed to respond to a state agency RFP with strict language service requirements, specific certifications for interpreter qualifications, and a tight submission window.

Analyzed the RFP requirements in full, structured a compliant response that addressed language pair coverage, turnaround standards, and quality assurance protocols. Assembled all required certifications and submitted via the state procurement portal on time.

Outcome

Proposal accepted and shortlisted — client advanced to the vendor evaluation stage for a multi-year language services contract.

Product Supply — Uniform & Equipment Bid

Uniform Supply Proposal for Municipal Department

A uniform and workwear supplier wanted to respond to a municipal department bid requiring detailed product specifications, unit pricing schedules, delivery timelines, and compliance with procurement formatting standards they were unfamiliar with.

Reviewed all technical specifications in the solicitation, developed a structured product proposal with compliant pricing schedules, organized required samples documentation, and formatted the full bid package to match the agency’s submission requirements.

Outcome

Bid submitted successfully and on time — client awarded a supply contract for departmental uniform procurement.

Professional Services — Federal RFQ

Administrative & Staffing Services RFQ Response

A professional services firm with no prior federal contracting experience needed to respond to a federal RFQ through SAM.gov. They lacked a capability statement, had no SAM.gov presence, and faced a short response window.

Guided the client through SAM.gov registration, developed a polished capability statement, wrote a compliant RFQ response tailored to the federal agency’s stated needs, and submitted the full package within the required timeframe.

Outcome

Client successfully entered the federal contracting space with an active SAM.gov profile and a targeted pipeline of matched opportunities.
